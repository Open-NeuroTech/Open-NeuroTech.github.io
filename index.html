About Open NeuroTech

We're a team of neuroscientists, engineers, and developers committed to democratizing neurotechnology.

Our Mission

Open NeuroTech was founded with the vision of making advanced neurotechnology accessible to everyone. We believe that open collaboration accelerates innovation in brain-computer interfaces and cognitive enhancement.

Our team combines decades of experience in neuroscience research with cutting-edge software engineering to create tools that push the boundaries of what's possible.
