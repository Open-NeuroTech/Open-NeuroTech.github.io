About Open NeuroTech

Founded on the principles of open science and academic collaboration, Open NeuroTech emerged from the recognition that high-quality neurotechnology should be accessible to researchers worldwide.

Our Mission

To democratize access to neurotechnology through affordable, high-quality hardware solutions and expert guidance, enabling breakthrough research in academic institutions globally.

Our Vision

A future where every neuroscience researcher has access to the tools and knowledge needed to push the boundaries of human understanding.

Our Values

Transparency, collaboration, scientific rigor, and accessibility drive everything we do. We believe in open science and reproducible research.

Academic Focus

Specifically tailored for the unique needs of academic research, with solutions designed for publication-quality data and reproducible methodologies.

Featured Projects
