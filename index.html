About Open NeuroTech LLC

We are a team of engineers committed to democratizing neurotechnology and strengthening the research community

Our Mission

Open NeuroTech was founded with the vision of making advanced neurotechnology treatments accessible to everyone. We believe that getting accessible technology in the hands of researchers and startups will accelerate innovation in bioelectronic medicine.

Our concept pushes the boundaries of what is possible across the open source, medical, and technical domains.
