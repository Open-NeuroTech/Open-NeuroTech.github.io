Why Open NeuroTech

The infrastructure layer neurotech has been missing

Every promising neuromodulation therapy faces the same wall: years of preclinical work, opaque regulatory paths, and a device ecosystem that wasn't built for their application. We remove that wall.

Compressing timelines

Where traditional paths to first-in-human take 5–10 years, our integrated platform — preclinical models, COSMIIC devices, regulatory support — cuts that to 18–36 months.

Massive addressable market

Over 3.8 billion people live with neurological conditions. Hundreds of academic labs and startups are pursuing neuromodulation therapies with no clear commercialization pathway. We are that pathway.

Defensible platform

Proprietary open-source hardware (COSMIIC), FDA IDE experience across multiple indications, and deep institutional relationships create a moat that takes years to replicate.

Multiple revenue streams

Services revenue (CRO contracts) funds operations today. Device licensing, pilot fees, and a growing product portfolio create durable, diversified ARR as the platform scales.

World-class team

30+ years of combined neurotech experience spanning device engineering, clinical operations, FDA regulatory strategy, and academic research translation.

De-risked by real traction

10 humans implanted, 3 active FDA IDEs, 16 paying pilot clients. This is not a pre-revenue concept — we have demonstrated the market, the product, and the execution capability.
