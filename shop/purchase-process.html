How it works

Three steps to your order

1. Request a quote

Use "Learn More" on any product or email us directly. Include billing contact and PO info if available.

2. We configure & quote

We check compatibility and include any necessary accessories or optional services such as sterilization and support contracts.

3. Invoice & payment

We issue an invoice and online payment portal to you and your institution's accounts payable. We follow up until payment clears.

FAQ

Common questions

How do I get a quote?

Click "Learn More" on any product card and select "Request Quote", or email us directly with the product SKU and your billing details.

Can you bill my university?

Yes. Provide your A/P contact and PO information and we'll assist with supplier onboarding if required by your institution.

What payment methods do you accept?

Institutional invoicing (A/P) and credit card are both supported. Custom arrangements may be possible for large orders.
